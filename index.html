About

Not the standard path,
which is the point.

I moved to Berlin to study Data Science, after 3.5 years inside Salesforce CRM systems for two global biotech clients, Genmab and Biogen, at HCL Technologies. I never went through a formal training phase. I was deployed straight into live delivery on day one, learned the platform through Trailhead while shipping real sprint commitments, and had to pass a client interview just to stay on the project.

That pattern, get thrown into something real, figure it out fast, leave it better documented than I found it, repeats everywhere in my work. It's why I went back to raw source data instead of trusting a report and found an error nobody else had caught. It's why I built an AI automation pipeline on my own time instead of waiting for someone to assign one.

Outside of CRM and data work, I've spent years as a freelance SQL and data analytics trainer, teaching 100+ students how to think in queries and structure, not just write them. Teaching is where I learned to explain complex things simply, a habit that shows up in every dashboard I build.
